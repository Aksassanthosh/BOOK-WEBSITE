Wings of fire

Author: APJ Abdul kalam

Wings of Fire is set on an unnamed planet[a] which features two known major continents, Pyrrhia and Pantala, and orbited by three moons. The two continents are predominantly inhabited by intelligent dragons that are grouped into ten tribes, seven of which inhabit Pyrrhia (MudWings, SeaWings, RainWings, SandWings, SkyWings, IceWings, NightWings) and three that inhabit Pantala (LeafWings, SilkWings, HiveWings); with a few exceptions, dragons from different tribes keep to themselves. (Hybrid dragons, while rare, are known to exist.) Each tribe is ruled by a dynasty of royal queens and lives in a habitat suited to its biological needs and abilities; most tribes have unique adaptations fitting for their respective habitats such as resistance to cold, the ability to breathe underwater, or camouflage scales. Both continents are also sparsely inhabited by humans (referred to as "scavengers" or "reading monkeys" by dragons, mostly the former term), who are viewed by most dragons as an inferior species or simply just another prey animal.[5] However, human-dragon interrelations and interactions are explored several times throughout the course of the series.[6] Though it is considered exceptionally rare, magic and magic-touched objects are known to exist in the dragon world, and animus dragons–capable of an intricate form of magic which comes at the cost of their souls–are largely responsible for such phenomena.
